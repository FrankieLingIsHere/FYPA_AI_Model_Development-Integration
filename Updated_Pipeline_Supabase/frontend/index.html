Welcome

Welcome to the PPE Safety Monitor Manual Handbook. This handbook provides guidance of CASM safety monitoring and some QnA to help users understand the system.

PPE Guidelines

  • Helmet must be worn
  • Safety vest required
  • Gloves for equipment handling

Live Monitoring

The live monitoring feature allows real-time detection of PPE violations using your webcam and YOLOv8 model. Once started, the system will process video frames in real-time and display bounding boxes around detected PPE items.

Instructions:

To start live monitoring, just click the "Start" button above.

  1. Click the "Start" button above to begin live monitoring
  2. Your webcam will activate and show the live feed
  3. YOLO will detect PPE in real-time with bounding boxes
  4. When violations are detected, they will be logged automatically
  5. Click "Stop" to end the monitoring session
