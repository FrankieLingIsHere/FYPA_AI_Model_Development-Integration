# AI Assistant Prompt Ideas

This document contains a list of potential user prompts for the AI assistant, categorized by system feature and user intent. The prompts are designed to cover a wide range of scenarios, user backgrounds, and natural language variations.

## 1. General & Conversational Prompts

These are common opening or general-knowledge questions.

*   **Simple Greetings & Inquiries:**
    *   "Hi"
    *   "Hello, are you there?"
    *   "What can you do?"
    *   "Tell me about this system."
    *   "How does this work?"
    *   "Can you help me?"

*   **System Overview:**
    *   "Give me a summary of the system's purpose."
    *   "What is CASM?"
    *   "Explain the PPE detection feature."

## 2. Live Monitoring & Status

Prompts related to real-time operational status.

*   **System Health:**
    *   "Is the system running?"
    *   "What's the current status?"
    *   "Show me the system health dashboard."
    *   "Are all services online?"
    *   "Any problems with the system right now?"

*   **Live Feed / Camera Status:**
    *   "Show me the live feed from camera 5."
    *   "Are there any active cameras?"
    *   "What's the status of the camera at the main entrance?"
    *   "Pull up the video stream for the construction zone."
    *   "Why is the camera in Area B offline?"

## 3. Violation & Compliance Reporting

Prompts for querying historical data about safety violations.

*   **General Violation Reports:**
    *   "Show me the latest violation report."
    *   "Any violations today?"
    *   "Generate a report of all violations this week."
    *   "I need a list of recent safety incidents."
    *   "Can I see the violations for yesterday?"

*   **Specific/Filtered Reports:**
    *   "Show me all helmet violations from last month."
    *   "List all incidents at the 'North Gate' camera."
    *   "How many vest violations were there on Monday?"
    *   "Generate a report for worker ID 12345."
    *   "Filter violations by date, from May 1st to May 5th."

*   **Compliance Rates:**
    *   "What's our overall PPE compliance rate for April?"
    *   "How compliant were we with safety standards last week?"
    *   "Show me a trend of compliance over the last quarter."

## 4. Device & System Management

Prompts for IT administrators or technical users managing the system hardware.

*   **Device Status:**
    *   "List all connected devices."
    *   "What's the IP address for camera 'CAM-007'?"
    *   "Is the edge streamer for the main gate running?"
    *   "Show me the logs for the device audit."

*   **Device Provisioning:**
    *   "Are there any pending devices waiting for approval?"
    *   "I need to provision a new camera. What's the process?"
    *   "Load the list of pending devices."
    *   "Authorize the new camera in the warehouse."

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support

Prompts for when users encounter issues.

*   **Error Diagnosis:**
    *   "I'm getting an error on the dashboard."
    *   "The live feed isn't loading, what's wrong?"
    *   "Why can't I generate a report?"
    *   "The system seems slow today."
    *   "My login isn't working."

*   **Guidance & Help:**
    *   "How do I export a report to PDF?"
    *   "Where can I find the documentation for setting up a new device?"
    *   "Walk me through the steps to review a violation."
    *   "What do the different colors on the dashboard mean?"

## 6. Data Analysis & Insights

More complex prompts that require data aggregation and interpretation.

*   **Trend Analysis:**
    *   "Which location has the most violations?"
    *   "What time of day do most incidents occur?"
    *   "Is there a correlation between worker shifts and compliance rates?"
    *   "Show me a chart of violations per day for the last month."

*   **Comparative Analysis:**
    *   "Compare the compliance rates of Zone A and Zone B."
    *   "Did we have more or fewer violations this month compared to last month?"
    *   "Which type of violation is most common: helmet or vest?"

## 7. Amateur / First-Time User Prompts

Prompts reflecting a user who is new to the system and unfamiliar with its terminology.

*   **Basic Orientation:**
    *   "I'm new to this, can you give me a quick tour?"
    *   "What am I looking at on this screen?"
    *   "Can you explain what this system does in simple terms?"
    *   "I don't understand what 'CASM' or 'PPE' means."

*   **Interpreting Information:**
    *   "I see a red alert, what does that mean? Is it bad?"
    *   "It says 'non-compliant', what does that mean?"
    *   "Someone isn't wearing their hard hat, is that what this system looks for?"
    *   "How can I tell if the construction site is safe right now?"
    *   "What's a 'compliance rate'? Is a high number good or bad?"

*   **Simple Data Queries:**
    *   "Did anything unsafe happen yesterday?"
    *   "Can you just show me if everyone is wearing their safety gear?"
    *   "I don't understand this report, can you summarize it for me?"
    *   "Is there a way to see all the times someone forgot their helmet?"

## 8. Random & Off-Topic Prompts

Prompts that are unrelated to the system's function, to test the assistant's ability to handle conversational tangents gracefully.

*   "What's the weather like today?"
*   "Tell me a joke."
*   "Who won the game last night?"
*   "Can you order me a pizza?"
*   "What is the capital of Mongolia?"
*   "I'm bored."

## 9. Amateur Users Accessing Tools (Natural Language)

Prompts where a non-technical user tries to perform an action without using formal commands or correct terminology.

*   **Accessing Live Feeds:**
    *   "I wanna see what's happening at the front gate."
    *   "Can you show me the cameras?"
    *   "Turn on the video for the warehouse."
    *   "Is there a camera looking at the west perimeter?"

*   **Generating Reports:**
    *   "I need to know how many times people forgot their helmets last week."
    *   "Can you make me a list of all the safety problems from yesterday?"
    *   "Just show me the bad stuff."
    *   "I need to prove to my boss that we're being safe, can you get me some numbers?"

*   **Troubleshooting:**
    *   "The screen is frozen."
    *   "Something's broken, it's not working."
    *   "I clicked the button and nothing happened."
    *   "The camera picture looks fuzzy."

## 10. Combined Requests

Prompts where the user asks for more than one thing at once, often in a single sentence.

*   "Show me the live feed and tell me if anyone is missing a helmet."
*   "Can you check the cameras, find any violations, and give me a summary?"
*   "I want the latest report plus the compliance rate for this week."
*   "Show me the camera status and also the last 10 incidents."
*   "Can you find yesterday's helmet violations and export them to CSV?"
*   "Give me the report, make it short, and tell me what matters most."
*   "Check if the system is online, and if it is, show me the dashboard."
*   "Pull up the warehouse camera and also tell me whether the edge device is running."
*   "I need a summary of violations and a breakdown by location."
*   "Show me everything from today, but only the important stuff."
*   "Find the problem cameras and then give me the latest alerts."
*   "Open the feed, check for safety issues, and tell me the time of each one."
*   "Can you export the filtered report and explain the numbers to me?"
*   "I want the chart and the raw data for the same report."
*   "Give me the live view first, then the report after that."

## 11. Filtering and Search Prompts

Prompts that ask the assistant to narrow results by time, place, device, or violation type.

*   "Only show helmet violations."
*   "Filter by last 24 hours."
*   "Show me incidents from the main gate camera only."
*   "List all violations in Zone A between Monday and Wednesday."
*   "Find all alerts from the night shift."

*   "I only want camera 2 and camera 4 results."
*   "Exclude false alarms."
*   "Filter the report by date and violation type."
*   "Show me just the warehouse incidents, not the office ones."
*   "Find all entries after 3 pm."
*   "Give me all results where the person is not wearing a vest."
*   "Only show me today's data."
*   "Search by location name and time range."
*   "Show me the ones marked urgent."
*   "Only include people missing helmets or vests."
*   "Filter for incidents with the west camera."
*   "Show me the last week but skip weekends."
*   "Find the records that mention the construction site entrance."
*   "Give me only the valid detections."

## 12. CSV Export and Download Prompts

Prompts that ask for export, download, spreadsheet, or raw-data output.

*   "Export this as CSV."
*   "Download the report as a spreadsheet."
*   "Can you save the filtered results into a CSV file?"
*   "I need the data in Excel format."
*   "Give me the raw table output."
*   "Can I get a CSV for all helmet violations this month?"
*   "Export only the filtered incidents."
*   "Make a downloadable file of the compliance report."
*   "Send me the results as a CSV and a summary."
*   "Put the camera list into a spreadsheet for me."
*   "I need a CSV export for my manager."
*   "Can you make a file I can open in Excel?"
*   "Export the analytics data, not just the summary."
*   "Download the full log if possible."
*   "Save the current view as a CSV and keep the filters."
*   "Export the filtered table with timestamps."
*   "I want the raw numbers, not the chart."
*   "Can you generate a CSV of the top violations?"
*   "Give me a spreadsheet with all camera statuses."
*   "Download everything from this report into one file."

*   "Show me which zone has the most incidents."
*   "Give me a simple dashboard view."
*   "Plot the weekly totals for me."

*   "Show me the analytics for this month."
*   "Which camera catches the most violations?"

*   "Compare the monthly totals side by side."
*   "Which location looks the safest overall?"
*   "Can you break down the issues by camera?"
*   "What does this report say in plain language?"

## 17. Natural Language Variations by User Type

Prompts from different kinds of users with different expectations and levels of formality.

*   **Site worker style:**
    *   "Yo, is the camera at the gate working?"
    *   "Any safety issues right now?"
    *   "Show me if people are missing gear."
    *   "Did somebody forget their vest again?"

*   **Supervisor style:**
    *   "Provide a quick compliance overview for this shift."
    *   "Summarize the main violations from the afternoon."
    *   "I need a filtered report for the warehouse area."
    *   "Export the site summary and include the top risks."

*   **Student / learner style:**
    *   "Can you explain what this chart means?"
    *   "I don't know how to read this report, can you help?"
    *   "What is the difference between a violation and an alert?"
    *   "How do I know if the site is compliant?"

*   **Very informal style:**
    *   "bro what is going on"
    *   "yo gimme the latest stuff"
    *   "pls fix the camera thing"
    *   "nah this ain't working"

## 19. Long, Detailed, or Verbose Prompts

Prompts where the user explains a lot, often with context, goals, and extra detail.

*   "I'm trying to understand how safe the site has been over the last few days, so can you look at the reports, pull out the main issues, and explain it to me in a way that's easy to follow?"
*   "I need a full breakdown of the violations from this week, including the location, the camera, the time, and whether the issue was related to helmets, vests, or something else."
*   "Could you check the live system status, confirm the cameras are online, and then give me a summary of anything that needs attention right now?"
*   "Please filter the incident history by the warehouse area, export the results to CSV, and include a short summary of the most common problems."
*   "I want the analytics for the last month, but not just raw numbers. Can you also tell me what the trends mean and whether things are getting better or worse?"
*   "Show me everything that happened yesterday, but group it by location and make it easy for someone who is not technical to understand."
*   "I don't really know the system yet, so please walk me through the dashboard, the camera status, and the report section one by one."
*   "Can you give me a report with the most important information first, then the detailed table, and finally a CSV download if possible?"
*   "I need to present this to my manager, so I need a clear summary, a chart, and the data behind it."
*   "Look through the compliance data and show me where the biggest problems are, with dates and locations if available."

*   "I'm a visitor and I just want to know if the site is safe."

## 34. Stress-Test and Edge Combination Prompts

Prompts designed to mix complexity, awkward wording, conflicting needs, and extra filler.

*   "Okay so first check if the main gate camera is working, then look for any helmet violations, then export only the ones from today, and after that give me a short plain-English summary."
*   "I need you to look at the last week, compare every camera, ignore false alarms, sort by time, and tell me which location is the biggest problem, all in one go."
*   "Can you get the report for the warehouse, the front gate, and the west side, but only for night shift, and then make it a CSV and a chart?"
*   "Please do the thing where you check the system, figure out what is broken, list the offline devices, and then tell me what I need to fix first."
*   "I want the analytics from the last month, but also the last two days, and I need both summaries, one detailed and one short, plus the raw file."
*   "Look at the data, split it by zone, camera, and date, then tell me the worst pattern and what time it usually happens."
*   "If there is a problem, show it; if there are many problems, group them; if there are no problems, just say that clearly."
*   "Can you handle all the reports, all the cameras, and all the export stuff, but only show me the important pieces?"

*   "Take whatever is relevant from this week, remove the noise, keep the safety stuff, and give me something I can paste into an email."

Prompts where the user gives unclear, conflicting, or self-contradictory instructions.
